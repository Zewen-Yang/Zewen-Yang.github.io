ResearchMate

ResearchMate — Find Your Next Collborator — is a project designed to facilitate collaborations between researchers by providing a platform for finding potential collaborators. The platform utilizes advanced techniques such as node and word embedding to recommend potential collaborators based on author similarity and co-author network in PubMed. By creating a collaborative filtering system that takes into account both collaboration patterns and author attributes, "Research Mate" aims to break down research barriers and connect researchers worldwide to enhance and accelerate research.

The controbuters also include Paraskevi Tassopoulou and Vladimir Angirov. More details about this project can be found in the slides.
