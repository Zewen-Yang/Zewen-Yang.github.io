DangAI

DangAI — Predicting Disease Spread — predict the number of dengue cases each week (in each location) based on environmental variables describing changes in temperature, precipitation, vegetation, and more. Dengue fever is a mosquito-borne disease that occurs in tropical and sub-tropical parts of the world. In mild cases, symptoms are similar to the flu: fever, rash, and muscle and joint pain. In severe cases, dengue fever can cause severe bleeding, low blood pressure, and even death. See more about the competition project.

Accurate dengue predictions would help public health workers and people around the world take steps to reduce the impact of these epidemics.
